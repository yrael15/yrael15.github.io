A Growing Metropolis

The Oliva Bay Metro area currently boasts a diverse population of just over 3.5 million people. From its roots as a sleepy fishing village, Oliva Bay has thrived through the years thanks to its location along magical leylines and the innovative combination of magic and science. Although the city faces unique challenges and dangers not seen by other major metro areas, Oliva Bay has always enjoyed protection from generations of Magical Girls.

Historic Oliva Bay University

Historic Oliva Bay University is a prestigious institution with a rich history dating back to the early 1900s. Situated directly on the Bay, this R1 Research University offers state-of-the-art facilities, esteemed faculty, and a vibrant campus life. With a commitment to academic excellence, Oliva Bay University consistently ranks among the top universities in the country for scientific research. Join the legacy of this remarkable institution and embark on a transformative educational journey.
