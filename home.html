About Bloom&Blight

We are a queer and trans led Girl by Moonlight actual play podcast following the titular magical girl team Bloom. After securing victory against their nemesis at a devastating cost, the strained team finds themselves drawn back together as whispers of evil begin again.

Our Setting

Our show is set in Oliva Bay, a west coast US city sitting on a vast bay featuring the historical Oliva Bay University. Although the city faces unique challenges and dangers not seen by other major metro areas, Oliva Bay has always enjoyed protection from generations of Magical Girls.
